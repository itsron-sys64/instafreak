# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 17, 2026

## About ClipThread
ClipThread is a Discord bot that detects Instagram Reel links and reposts them as embedded videos inside Discord servers.

## Data We Collect
ClipThread does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data.

- We do not log usernames, user IDs, or message content.
- Instagram links are processed in real time and immediately discarded after the video is sent.
- No database or persistent storage is used.

## Third Party Services
ClipThread interacts with:
- **Instagram** — to fetch publicly available Reel videos.
- **Discord** — to receive and send messages in servers where the bot is installed.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these platforms.

## Children's Privacy
ClipThread does not knowingly collect any information from users under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the bot constitutes acceptance of any changes.
