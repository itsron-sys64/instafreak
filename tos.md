## About ClipThread
ClipThread is a Discord bot that automatically detects Instagram Reel links shared in Discord servers and reposts them as embedded videos for easier viewing.

- You will not use ClipThread to download or redistribute private, copyrighted, or protected content without permission.

- You will not abuse, spam, or attempt to exploit the bot in any way.
- You understand that ClipThread only works with publicly accessible Instagram Reels.
